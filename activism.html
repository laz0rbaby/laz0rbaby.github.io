Activism

For the last 3 years I have been volunteer tutoring through the NYC branch of the educational justice nonprofit Petey Greene Program. PGP has matched me with programs such as Exalt Youth Program and the John Jay Navigator Program.

Additionally, I cook for a mutual aid based in Bed-Stuy and East Harlem called We The People. The images on the right showcase some of the massive (40+ serving) portions that I have cooked for WTP. For more information on how to get free hot meals in East Harlem, check out the WTP website!
